001 Markets
Most people think about what something is worth today. Second-level thinking asks: what do others think it's worth, and why are they wrong? The gap between those two questions is where every interesting investment lives.

After rereading Howard Marks. The insight isn't about being contrarian — it's about the meta-layer. Price is an opinion about an opinion. Most investors forget there are two layers.

Personal note, after The Most Important Thing

003 Literature

Finished this at 2am. The book isn't about alienation — it's about the exhaustion of performing normalcy for a world that doesn't know it's a performance.

004 Fragrance

Jubilation XXV opens with a kind of ceremonial darkness — labdanum, oud, resins that smell like they've been burning in a room for centuries. It's not a fragrance you wear. It's a weight you carry. Some days that's exactly what you need.

005 Machines

The GT3 Touring removes the wing. That's the whole choice. Every other GT car announces its intentions — the Touring whispers them. Same logic as wearing something only a few people recognize as exceptional. The restraint is the statement.

On the Porsche 911 GT3 Touring

007 Markets

Precious metals have no earnings, no management, no moat — and that's precisely the point. Sometimes the most honest store of value is the one that makes no promises at all.

008 Literature
I laid my heart open to the gentle indifference of the universe. To feel it so like myself, indeed so brotherly, made me realize that I'd been happy, and that I was happy still.

Meursault's peace, at the end. Not nihilism — something closer to release. He stopped performing and the world didn't punish him. The world was simply indifferent, and that indifference turned out to feel like grace.

Camus, The Stranger
009 Geopolitics

Zeihan's core argument is demographic, not ideological: populations that aren't replacing themselves can't sustain the supply chains they built when they were young. The next twenty years aren't about who wins a trade war. They're about who can still find workers to fight one.

After The End of the World Is Just the Beginning
